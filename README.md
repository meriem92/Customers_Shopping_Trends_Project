# Customer Shopping Trends Project

 
Link to Dataset --> https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/iamsouravbanerjee/customer-shopping-trends-dataset/

Data Visualization, Data Analysis, Excel, SQl


# About
This dataset encompasses various features related to customer shopping preferences, gathering essential information for businesses seeking to enhance their understanding of their customer base. 

# Objective
Understand customer behavior and purchasing patterns so that businesses can make informed decisions,optimize product offerings and engance customer's satisfaction.

# Conclusion
* More male customers than females
* Most popular category sold is Clothing
* Best payment method preferred by customers is Paypal
* Customers prefer free shipping
* Spring is the season customers buy the most
* Customers in their 46-59 buys the most
